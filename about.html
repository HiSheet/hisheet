✨ About HiSheet – The Story Behind the Sheets

HiSheet isn’t just about spreadsheets — it’s about making data simple, powerful, and stress-free for everyone.

I’m Muhammad Hashir, the Founder of HiSheet, an Excel freelancer from Karachi, Pakistan, trained at Learnify Academy. I started HiSheet with a simple idea:

“Most people struggle with Excel… I want to change that.”

I’ve worked with students, freelancers, small business owners, and professionals — helping them transform messy spreadsheets into clean, meaningful files that save time and improve decisions.

💡 Why I Created HiSheet

There are many tools out there, but Excel remains one of the most essential — and underused — ones. That’s where HiSheet comes in: to bridge the gap between people and their data.

  • ✔️ Listening to your needs
  • ✔️ Delivering clean, structured, and automated spreadsheets
  • ✔️ Keeping everything confidential and client-friendly

🌍 My Vision

“To make Excel accessible, easy, and professional — for everyone, everywhere.”

Whether it’s a budget planner, an academic mark sheet, or a business dashboard — I want every client to feel confident using their data.
